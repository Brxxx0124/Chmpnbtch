CHAMPION BIOTECH AND PHARMA CORP.

Champion Biotech and Pharma Corporation, a duly registered corporation with the Securities and Exchange Commission, located in San Juan, Metro Manila, was established in June of 1997. It is a comprehensive company integrating research and development, which specializes in intravenous fluids, pharmaceutical drugs and injectables, medical devices, healthcare machines, and rehabilitation and wellness equipment. With its more than two decades of experience as a pioneer in Filipino healthcare, it has earned for itself the distinction of having credibility, innovation, and high quality for its products.​

The company’s products are developed and marketed strictly in accordance with Philippine FDA standards. It likewise boasts of a very comprehensive and complete line of Certificate of Product Registrations to go with its roster of products. The company keeps close attention to new sciences, materials and technology in related field in the world at all times and adheres to the business principle of “quality first, our service to the Filipino”.​

At present, our products are marketed throughout the Philippine archipelago with the partnership of premier drugstore chains, government and private hospitals, our elite dealers and distributors all over the Philippines with our signature warm pre-sales, sales, and after sales service through our highly committed and competent medical and technical team. Our commitment- HIGH QUALITY, PERFECT SERVICE, PROMPT DELIVERY, COMPETITIVE PRICE.
